Quepos in Costa Rica

i visited this place in july, 2019

Quepos is paradise for anyone looking for a culturally immersive stay. It has all of the local flavor of an authentic Costa Rican town, while still providing top notch bars, restaurants, live music, and stellar views. I had a great time with my family sliding down the water fall and feeding monkeys.
